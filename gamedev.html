AssCat

#TextJam2015

Asscat is a HTML5 text-based adventure game about an ass trapped in the body of a cat. Told from the first-person perspective of a cat, players must make decisions that impact not only their life but the lives of their owner’s, as well. It was created by Dustin Triplett on #InternationalCatDay for #TextJam2015.
