The Skinny

Hello! My name is Dustin Triplett and I am a St. Louis based writer, game developer, web designer, and overall Swiss army knife of creativity.

It is my dream to write and design creative solutions that improve the quality of life for countless people. I believe in solving challenges by embracing exciting new perspectives and by constantly honing my skills and developing new ones on my quest for world domination.

If you are interested in working with me, please send me an email using the contact form on the right. I'll get back to you as soon as possible and gladly answer any questions or project proposals you may have.

What I Do

Design : I firmly believe that creative design can do more than just sell a product. When done correctly, it can change our hearts, minds, and habits for the best.

Web Design : The internet can be an awful, ugly place. As a result, I love to geek out by building engaging websites that are fun to use, easy on the eyes, and a drastic contrast from regurgitated websites masquerading as “premium themes”.

Illustration : I believe that illustration is the most compelling method to turn heads and convey a message. To get these messages across, I specialize in graphic illustration.

Video Games : As a passionate gamer with a strong technical background, it was only a matter of time before I started designing and developing video games independently.
